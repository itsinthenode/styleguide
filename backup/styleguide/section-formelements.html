6 #FormElements Form Elements

6.1 #FormElements.Inputs Inputs

Example
Something is wrong. 
 <div class="form-field">
  <label for="text">Test</label>
  <input type="text" placeholder="Placeholder" id="text" />
 </div>
 <div class="form-field">
  <label for="text" class="disabled">Test</label>
  <input type="text" placeholder=":disabled" disabled="disabled" id="text" />
 </div>
 <div class="form-field--has-errors">
  <label for="text">Test</label>
  <input aria-invalid="true" type="text" value="text" id="text" />
  <span>Something is wrong.</span>
 </div>
